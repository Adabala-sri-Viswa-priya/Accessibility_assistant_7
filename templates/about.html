The Challenge We Address

Digital content is central to education, work, and daily life, yet many websites and apps remain difficult or impossible for people with disabilities. Our challenge was to build an assistant that bridges this gap.

Visual Impairments

Text-to-Speech (TTS) technology converts written content into clear spoken audio, enabling visually impaired users to access digital information effortlessly.

Hearing Impairments

Speech-to-Text (STT) transcribes spoken words into written text in real time, making communication accessible for those with hearing difficulties.

Motor Impairments

Voice navigation allows users with motor disabilities to control devices and applications using simple voice commands, eliminating the need for precise mouse or keyboard input.

Our Comprehensive Solution

Our Accessibility Assistant combines cutting-edge AI technologies to create a seamless, inclusive digital experience. Three powerful features work together to break down accessibility barriers.

Advanced Text-to-Speech

Intelligent Speech-to-Text
